Imagine never forgetting a brilliant idea you read, a code snippet you typed, or a crucial document you browsed. **PureMemory** gives your AI assistants a photographic memory of your digital life—securely, privately, and automatically. It silently captures your desktop and browser activity, distills it into organized thoughts using powerful AI pipelines, and builds a deeply personal, long-term context for your AI interactions. 

Stop repeating yourself to your AI. Give it the context it deserves, and experience a truly personalized assistant that already knows what you are working on.

## 🌟 The Core Concept

At its heart, PureMemory is a highly optimized, privacy-first telemetry system designed to bridge the gap between your daily workflows and your AI tools. 

Here is how it works:

1. **Observe (The Collector)**: A phenomenally lightweight, cross-platform agent and browser extension silently monitor your active windows, typed text, clipboard, and browser history. Operating on less than 20MB of RAM, it runs invisibly without killing your battery.
2. **Distill (The Pipeline)**: Your raw activity data is securely transferred to your private server where an advanced AI pipeline reads, summarizes, and categorizes your activity into meaningful, long-term memory embeddings.
3. **Recall (The MCP Server)**: Your AI agents can seamlessly query these memories through a built-in MCP (Model Context Protocol) server. When you ask a question, the AI autonomously retrieves exactly what you've been researching, reading, or coding over the past days.

## ✨ Why PureMemory?

- **True AI Personalization**: Make any LLM feel like it truly knows you. Your AI models get immediate, secure access to your recent context and long-term interests.
- **Privacy By Design**: Passwords, banking sites, and private directories are strictly blacklisted. Sensitive data is aggressively filtered and discarded at the edge before it ever reaches a database.
- **Unobtrusive & Invisible**: No heavy, bloated applications. The core collector is built natively in Go, guaranteeing an entirely seamless desktop experience.
- **Offline Resiliency**: Working on a plane or have a poor network? PureMemory holds your data in an ultra-fast local SQLite buffer and synchronizes safely the moment your server is back online.
